# CoachCarts Lab
CoachCarts Lab is a nonprofit chess organization providing free lessons, training plans, study materials, tournaments, and advice from experienced players. Our mission is to make quality chess education accessible to everyone while building a welcoming community where players can learn, improve, and connect.

CoachCartsLab has currently 400+ members being impacted, and it tends to expand even more people.

I'm Viet Anh Ho. I'm the moderator and web developer of CoachCartsLab. CoachCartsLab is a nonprofit chess organization dedicated to making high-quality chess education accessible to everyone, regardless of financial circumstances. Our mission is to prove that players do not need expensive coaching or paid programs to improve at chess. Through free lessons, tournaments, study materials, mentorship, and community support, we strive to create a welcoming environment where players of all skill levels can learn, improve, and connect with others who share a passion for chess. So far, CoachCarts Lab has made a direct impact on 400+ members of the chess community, with the goal of becoming one of the most beneficial free chess organizations in the world of chess. One of our flagship offerings is a set of free weekly lessons hosted live in our Discord server, open to all members regardless of rating or experience.
We have around 200+ members from all around the world in Discord and are intended to expand much more in the near future. Our organization has so far had some free weekly lessons run live in our Discord server, covering tactics, openings, middlegame, and endgame topics. We have also created some regular Chess.com and Lichess club tournaments and arena events for members of all rating levels. We created a really active Discord community where players ask questions, share games for analysis, and find training partners, and so far we have had over 200 members in the Discord group. There is also a growing library of community-created study materials and educational presentations.
